The Process

How It Works

From your first enquiry to the final nail — we handle everything so you can focus on running your business.

We deliver, install, and style every piece ourselves. You don't lift a finger. We don't leave until you're completely delighted with how your space looks.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a decoration project take?

It depends on the scope. A single-room decoration typically takes 2–3 weeks from approval to installation. Larger projects (multiple rooms) may take 4–6 weeks. We'll give you an accurate timeline in your quote.

Do you service all cities in Andhra Pradesh?

Yes! We service all major cities in Andhra Pradesh including Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada, Guntur, Tirupati, Nellore, Rajahmundry, Kakinada, and more. For smaller towns, please contact us to confirm availability.

Can I choose my own designs or themes?

Absolutely. During the consultation, we discuss your brand, colour palette, and aesthetic preferences in detail. We can customise designs completely to match your vision — or suggest curated options that complement your space.

What is included in the "installation"?

We handle all wall-mounting, hanging hardware, levelling, and final styling. We bring all necessary tools. You don't need to arrange anything — just be present (or have a staff member available) on installation day.

Is the site visit really free with no obligation?

Yes, 100% free and zero obligation. We visit, discuss your ideas, take measurements, and provide a detailed quote. You are under no pressure to proceed. We believe in earning your trust through quality, not pressure.

How do payments work?

Once you approve the quote, we collect a 50% advance to begin production. The remaining 50% is due on installation day — after you've seen the final result and are happy with it. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and cash.
