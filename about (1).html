Who We Are

Threads of Tradition,
Woven with Purpose

ALVOR was born from a deep love of traditional Indian embroidery and the rich textile heritage of Andhra Pradesh. What started as a personal passion for handcrafted art evolved into a mission — to preserve these timeless techniques and bring them into the homes and spaces of those who truly appreciate craftsmanship.

Every piece we create is a dialogue between the past and the present. Our artisans draw from generations of knowledge, using traditional motifs — lotus blooms, peacock feathers, geometric mandalas — and elevate them with premium framing, archival materials, and meticulous attention to detail.

We believe art should not live in galleries alone. It belongs in your home, your café, your workspace — anywhere that deserves beauty with meaning. That is why every ALVOR piece is crafted not just to be seen, but to be felt.

How We Work

From Sketch to Wall

Every ALVOR piece goes through a meticulous four-step process to ensure it reaches you as a true work of art.

01

Design & Sketch

Each piece begins with a detailed sketch — drawing from traditional Indian motifs like lotus, peacock, and geometric mandalas, adapted to the frame dimensions.

02

Hand Embroidery

Our artisans bring the sketch to life using premium threads, silk, and metallic accents — each stitch placed with care, often taking 20–80 hours per piece.

03

Premium Framing

The finished embroidery is mounted on archival board and enclosed in solid wood frames with UV-protective glass — ensuring your art lasts generations.

04

Quality & Dispatch

Every piece is inspected, wrapped in protective packaging, and hand-delivered across Andhra Pradesh with care. Custom orders receive a signed certificate.
